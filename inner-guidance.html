Inner Guidance vs Intuition

March 2026 • Shoonya Foundation
← Back to Articles

What is intuition? Is it a subtle movement of intelligence, or is it the mind calculating the most favorable outcome and presenting it as wisdom?

There seems to be a difference between intuition and inner guidance. Intuition is often mixed with memory, fear, preference, and self-protection. It can feel compelling because it is tied to outcome.

Inner guidance is quieter. It does not threaten. It does not bargain. It does not say that your life will collapse if you do not obey it.

It is more like a whisper in presence. You may follow it or not follow it, and still something deeper remains untouched. Love remains. Compassion remains. Presence remains.

That is why inner guidance feels complete in itself. It does not come from fear, and it does not produce fear. It simply illuminates what is true in this moment and leaves the rest to you.

Perhaps real inquiry begins when we start seeing how often fear dresses itself as wisdom, and how quietly truth arrives when the mind is no longer struggling to secure itself.
