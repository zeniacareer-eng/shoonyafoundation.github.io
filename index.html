About Shoonya

Shoonya Foundation is a space for sincere inquiry into life, mind, truth, and freedom. Through satsang, spiritual reading circles, reflection, and dialogue, we come together not to accumulate beliefs, but to look deeply, honestly, and quietly.

Shoonya means emptiness — not as absence, but as spaciousness. In that inner space, clarity, compassion, and right action can naturally emerge.

Our Vision

To nurture a community rooted in awareness, inquiry, and inward transformation. We believe real change begins when one starts observing deeply, questioning sincerely, and living from clarity rather than fear.

What We Offer

  • Satsang gatherings
  • Bhagavad Gita reading circles
  • Self-inquiry and reflection groups
  • Dialogue-centered spiritual community
  • Simple spaces for silence, learning, and connection

Join the Community

If you feel drawn toward sincerity, inquiry, and inner understanding, you are welcome here. This is an open space for those who wish to read, reflect, ask, listen, and grow together.
